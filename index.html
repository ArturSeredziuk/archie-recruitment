How it works

From brief to started.

A clear, four-step process — no black box, no chasing updates. You see every candidate, every stage, in real time through our ATS.

01
Intake call
30 minutes. We dig into the role, the team, the must-haves and the deal-breakers. Nothing leaves this call vague.
02
Sourcing & screening
Warm database first, targeted outreach second. AI scores fit; we screen attitude, motivation, and notice period.
03
Shortlist delivered
3–5 fully prepared candidates with rewritten CVs and our notes. You review, we coordinate the interviews.
04
Offer & onboarding
We negotiate, close, and stay in touch through the first weeks. Replacement guarantee if it doesn't stick.

The deal

How we work together.

What I offer — why me

  • 5+ years on the Dutch market. Deep network, real placements, real retention — not just a fresh LinkedIn account.
  • Speed, accuracy, and clear communication. First shortlist in 1–2 days, status updates without you asking.
  • Sharp tools. Custom ATS built around my workflow, plus an AI agent that handles the noise so I can focus on the human side.
  • Soft skills that build real relationships. Candidates stay warm and pick me as their personal recruiter — that's why they pick up the phone next time.
  • Red-flag detection. Patterns repeat. I spot them early — before they cost you a no-show or a 3-week walk-out.
  • A strong, warm database. Curated candidates from Poland, Romania, Spain and Portugal — already screened, already trusting.

What I ask for

  • Open communication and timely feedback. If I send a shortlist, I need to know what landed and what didn't.
  • A detailed brief on the role. Not "I need a welder."Better: "I need a TIG welder with 5+ years' experience, some pipe RVS welding for the food industry."
  • Fee: €1.50–2.00 per hour worked. Within the standard NL outsourced-recruitment range — billed as the candidate logs hours, so we're aligned on retention from day one.
  • Minimum 1,500 hours per placement. Long enough to be a real role, not a stop-gap — below this the recruitment effort doesn't pay off for either of us.
  • 7-day invoice terms. Invoice issued when the candidate starts and paid within a week — keeps cash flow predictable on both sides, no chasing.
  • 3-month fair rule. If a candidate is in your database but hasn't been contacted in the last 3 months and is cold, they count as mine.
